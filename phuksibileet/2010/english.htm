In English

Kick-off your easter at the classiest student party of the year: Club Tease at the Nude Lounge & Club on March 31st from 9pm to 4am!

Age limit 18 years.

Go see the Facebook event

Location and Transport

Club Tease will take place at the Nude Lounge & Club
Kaisaniemenkatu 2 A
Helsinki

Check out their website or find the place on the map.

Buses to Otaniemi

Extra buses to Otaniemi leave at 3am and 4am from Rautatientori (east side of the railway station). The bus is included in the ticket price. Seats are booked upon arrival to Nude.

Tickets

Tickets can be bought beforehand for 5€ at the following locations:

  • Helsinki University of Technology, Main building: 23rd to 25th of March and 29rd to 31st of March from 10am to 2pm at the lobby.
  • Helsinki School of Economics 29th of March from 10am to 2pm at the lobby.
  • University of Helsinki, Porthania building 24th of March from 11am to 1pm at the lobby.

Tiketti

You can also buy tickets for 6€ from Tiketti ticket offices or the Tiketti website when the tickets become available.

At the door

Tickets can be bought at the door for 7€.
